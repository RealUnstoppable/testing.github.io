Welcome to our Church.

Dear friend, No matter who you are, what your nationality is, or the color of your skin, have you ever asked yourself, "Why was I born into this world? What is my purpose? Where am I going? And what will happen after I die?" To all these questions, there is an answer! It is found in the book of books, the Holy Bible. We have found these answers for ourselves and would like to share them with you as well. God is the creator of this universe, everything and everyone in it. Jesus Christ is God's son. He died for our sins so that we may have everylasting life in Heaven. He left us with the Holy Spirit, who can help you survive in this world and bring you closer to God. If you have never read the Bible, please find a copy, many are given out for free, and read it for yourself. Our desire is that you will have peace and joy in your heart that only can be given to you by God. Only God and his Holy Word can change your life, your thoughts, your desires, and lead you onto the path of righteousness. Find time to carefully read each sentance, each verse, each chapter to fully understand why you were born, why you life, and what will happen when you die. Don't forget that God is love, He loves you, He is calling for you, come to Him.
